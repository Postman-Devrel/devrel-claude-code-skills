# Branded Terms

This is a document for all "branded terms" used in outward communication with Postman customers. Branded terms are keywords that we want to have associated with the larger Postman brand. When used in customer-facing communication, they should be capitalized.

This document is co-owned by @Rebecca Johnston-Gilbert and @Jonathan Konrath. If you're unsure about branded terms then work with your Product Managers and UX Writers. Always involve your UX Writer so that they can update the necessary docs.
